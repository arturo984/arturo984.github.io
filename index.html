The Challenge

FMW had two decades of expert fabrication work but no digital presence that reflected it. Contractors and designers evaluating vendors had no easy way to assess their capabilities, and FMW had no tool to support the word-of-mouth referrals that drive their business.

Our Approach

We repositioned FMW's digital presence to target commercial clients by making their specialty fabrication capabilities immediately clear to contractors and designers. The strategy centered on building a shareable tool that shortens the path from referral to signed contract.

What We Delivered

Website Strategy
Audience research, content strategy, and site architecture
Custom Design
Bespoke visual design built to showcase fabrication work
Webflow Development
Fully responsive build with CMS for ongoing updates
SEO Foundations
Technical SEO, metadata, and search visibility strategy
Newsletter Migration
Mailchimp migration with subscriber list and templates
Marketing Automation
Zapier integrations connecting forms, CMS, and email

The Outcome

FMW now has a professional digital presence that makes their 20+ years of expertise immediately visible to potential commercial clients. Contractors and designers can evaluate their capabilities at a glance, and the site serves as a shareable tool that shortens the distance between referral and signed contract.
